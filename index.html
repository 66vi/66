Common optical LVA

 include:

See the Latest Devices

Magnifiers 

These sometimes have their own illumination and are either battery‐powered or rechargeable from mains electricity. They may be hand‐held or mounted on a stand or on spectacles;

Telescopes

 for work where the reading matter is distant, a telescope can be mounted on a spectacle frame. This gives a longer working distance, although less can be seen at one time because the field is necessarily restricted. Telescopes may also be hand‐held. 

Electronic devices

Electronic aids include primarily closed circuit television and other readers incorporating a monitor or a liquid‐crystal display (LCD) screen, which provide improved contrast and magnification.

About 66vision.org

66vision.org is the info website to help you find the suitable Low Vision devices according to the WHO's Priority Assistant Product list which is recommended by World Health Organisation and International Agency Prevent Blindness.

Our offices are located in Hong Kong & Dongguan. We have more than 10 years experience to supply different type of low vision devices.
